## Design Rules

- Write one measurable learning goal per activity.
- Keep feedback brief, specific, and corrective.
- Avoid trick questions unless the misconception is the learning target.
- Use unlimited attempts for practice activities.
- Use gradebook scoring only when the activity is intended as assessment.
- Add alt text for all meaningful images.
- Make completion requirements clear in Moodle.

## Suggested Moodle Settings

- Practice activities: unlimited attempts, highest grade or no grade.
- Required checks: pass grade 70-80%, completion when passing grade is achieved.
- Reflection activities: completion by viewing or manual completion.
- Content bank reuse: store master H5P files in the Moodle content bank, then link or copy into course pages.
