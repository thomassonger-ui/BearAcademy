REACHING OUT IS NOT A SIGN OF WEAKNESS.

The broker and team lead exist to support you. The cost of NOT calling is always higher than the cost of calling.

THE BEAR TEAM RULE:
• If you're 80% sure → call anyway and confirm
• If you're guessing → call before acting
